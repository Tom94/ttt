The quantum drive hummed beneath the deck plates. Stars stretched into brilliant lines of light as the ship accelerated past lightspeed. Captain Chen checked the navigation display, plotting their course through the Carina nebula. Earth was fifty years behind them now, a blue marble lost in the cosmic sea. The crew slept in their cryo pods while automated systems maintained their endless trajectory toward Proxima Centauri. Only the soft beeping of instruments broke the silence of deep space.
